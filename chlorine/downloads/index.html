Verifying Downloads

To verify the integrity of your downloaded ISO file, you can use the SHA256 checksum files provided alongside each ISO on our download portal. Here's how to verify the checksum:

On Linux:

sha256sum -c chlorine-1.0-kde-x86_64.iso.sha256

Or manually compare:

sha256sum chlorine-1.0-kde-x86_64.iso

On macOS:

shasum -a 256 -c chlorine-1.0-kde-x86_64.iso.sha256

Or manually compare:

shasum -a 256 chlorine-1.0-kde-x86_64.iso

On Windows:

certUtil -hashfile chlorine-1.0-kde-x86_64.iso SHA256

Compare the output with the SHA256 checksum provided on the download portal. If they match, your download is valid.
